Oversee a growing colony of settlers in a world of magic. Assign priorities, build structures, channel leylines, brew potions, and survive the seasons.

Controls: WASD/Arrows=Pan | B=Build | Z=Zone | G=Gather | P=Priorities
C=Craft | R=Research | T=Tame | I=Inventory | ,=Settings
Tab=Toggle Chop/Mine | Space=Pause | </>=Speed | +/-=Zoom
Click=Select/Place | Click+Drag=Area select | Right-click=Cancel | Esc=Close/Normal
